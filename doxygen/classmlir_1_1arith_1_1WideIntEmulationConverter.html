Detailed Description

Converts integer types that are too wide for the target by splitting them in two halves and thus turning into supported ones, i.e., i2*N --> iN, where N is the widest integer bitwidth supported by the target.

Currently, we only handle power-of-two integer types and support conversions of integers twice as wide as the maxium supported by the target. Wide integers are represented as vectors, e.g., i64 --> vector<2xi32>, where the first element is the low half of the original integer, and the second element the high half.
