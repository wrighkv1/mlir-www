Constructor: take an array of tensors inputs, on which the generated loops will iterate on.

The index of the tensor in the array is also the tensor id (tid) used in related functions. If isSparseOut is set, loop emitter assume that the sparse output tensor is empty, and will always generate loops on it based on the dim sizes.
