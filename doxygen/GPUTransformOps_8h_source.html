36 /// Searches `scf.foreach_thread` ops nested under `target` and maps each such
37 /// op to GPU threads. Mapping is one-to-one and the induction variables of
38 /// `scf.foreach_thread` are rewritten to gpu.thread_id according to the
39 /// thread_dim_apping attribute. Sibling `scf.foreach_thread` are supported in
40 /// which case, the union of the number of threads is computed and may result in
41 /// predication. Dynamic, `scf.foreach_thread` trip counts are currently not
42 /// supported. Dynamic block dim sizes are currently not supported.
43 DiagnosedSilenceableFailure mapNestedForeachToThreadsImpl(
44  RewriterBase &rewriter, Operation *target,
45  const SmallVectorImpl<int64_t> &blockDim, bool syncAfterDistribute,
46  llvm::Optional<TransformOpInterface> transformOp,
47  const ArrayRef<DeviceMappingAttrInterface> &threadMappingAttributes);

53 /// dim sizes are currently not supported.
