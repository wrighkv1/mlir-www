29 /// Try to eliminate tensor::EmptyOps inside `op`.
30 ///
31 /// * `rewriteFunc` generates the replacement for the tensor::EmptyOp.
32 /// * Only tensor::EmptyOps that are anchored on a matching OpOperand as per
33 /// `anchorMatchFunc` are considered. "Anchored" means that there is a path
34 /// on the reverse SSA use-def chain, starting from the OpOperand and always
35 /// following the aliasing OpOperand, that eventually ends at a single
36 /// tensor::EmptyOp.

38  bufferization::AnalysisState &state,
39  AnchorMatchFn anchorMatchFunc,
40  RewriteFn rewriteFunc);
